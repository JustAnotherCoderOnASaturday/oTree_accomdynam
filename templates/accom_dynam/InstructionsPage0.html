Based on your previous answers, the computer has created two models: one for your preferences over movie genres and one for your preferences over pizza types.

In Stage 3:

  • You will interact with 5 partners (one at a time).
  • Each interaction will be carried out through a game that will last about 12 minutes.
  • During the game, you will learn about your partner's preferences over either pizza types or movie genres.
  • With help and information provided by the computer, your task is to create a model of his/her preferences.
  • Your partner will simultaneously be learning about and creating a model of your preferences.
  • At the end of the experiment, one of the five partner interactions will be randomly chosen for payment.
